About Us

Basil Bistro, located in the heart of Kompally, is a vibrant multi-cuisine restaurant that blends rich Indian flavors with popular global favorites. From aromatic Biryanis to delicious oven-baked Pizzas and creamy Pastas, the bistro offers a diverse menu designed to delight every palate.
